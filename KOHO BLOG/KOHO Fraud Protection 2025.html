Your First Line of Defence: A Smarter Account Structure

Before we even get to the high-tech features, it’s important to understand how KOHO’s basic structure acts as a powerful security tool. Your KOHO account is a reloadable prepaid Mastercard, which means it’s not directly connected to your main bank account where you might keep the bulk of your savings.

Think of your main bank account as a vault. You want to keep it as secure as possible. Your KOHO account functions as your everyday wallet. You can load it with the money you need for the week's groceries, your monthly subscriptions, or your travel budget.

This separation creates a "firewall" for your finances. In the unlikely event that your KOHO card details are ever compromised, a fraudster could only access the funds you’ve loaded onto that card. Your main savings account remains untouched and secure. This simple design significantly reduces your risk compared to using a traditional debit card, which is directly linked to your entire chequing account balance.

Real-Time Notifications: Your Personal Watchdog

One of the most effective ways to spot fraud is to catch it the second it happens. With traditional banking, you might not notice an unauthorized charge until days later when you check your statement, by which time a fraudster could have made several more transactions.

KOHO gives you instant awareness with real-time notifications.

  • How it Works: The moment a transaction is made with your KOHO card—whether it’s you buying lunch or someone else trying to make an unauthorized purchase—you get an immediate notification on your phone.
  • Why it Matters: This instant alert system means you are always the first to know what’s happening with your account. If you get a notification for a purchase you don’t recognize, you can take action immediately. It’s like having a personal security guard for your wallet, watching over every transaction 24/7.

Virtual Cards: The Ultimate Tool for Safe Online Shopping

Shopping online is a huge part of modern life, but it also carries risks. Entering your card details on a website can feel like a gamble, as data breaches are becoming more common.

KOHO offers an elegant and powerful solution to this problem with Virtual Cards.

  • How it Works: A Virtual Card is a unique 16-digit card number, expiry date, and CVV that exists only within your KOHO app. It is completely separate from your physical card. You can use this virtual number for all your online shopping and subscriptions.
  • The Security Benefit: By using a Virtual Card online, you never expose the details of your physical card to merchants. If a website you shop at suffers a data breach, your physical card remains secure and can still be used for in-store purchases without any worry. You can also lock your Virtual Card at any time, giving you another layer of control over your online spending.
