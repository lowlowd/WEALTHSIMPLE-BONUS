The Challenge of Family Finances

Every family’s financial situation is unique, but the challenges are often universal. Keeping track of who spent what, sticking to a household budget, and saving for the future requires teamwork and clear communication. Traditional joint bank accounts can help, but they often fall short. They show you transactions, but they don’t give you the insights you need to make better decisions. You see that money was spent at a supermarket, but you don't have an easy way to track your total grocery spending for the month against your budget.

KOHO is designed to fill this gap. It’s a transparent, easy-to-use system that gives your family the clarity and control you need to manage your money as a team.

Track Your Household Spending with Ease

One of the biggest sources of financial stress for families is not knowing where the money goes. Between groceries, gas, kids’ soccer fees, and the occasional pizza night, expenses can add up fast. KOHO brings simplicity and clarity to your household spending.

  • Real-Time Spending Insights: When you use your KOHO card, you get an instant notification on your phone. The app automatically categorizes the purchase, so you can see at a glance how much you’re spending on "Groceries," "Transportation," or "Entertainment." This shared visibility helps everyone in the family stay on the same page.
  • A Clear Picture of Your Budget: No more guessing games at the end of the month. The app provides clear, visual summaries of your spending. You can easily see if you’re staying on track with your household budget and identify areas where you might be overspending. This data empowers you to have productive conversations about money and make adjustments as a team.

Imagine knowing exactly how much your family has spent on takeout this month with just a few taps. That’s the kind of powerful insight KOHO provides, helping you make smarter decisions together.

Say Goodbye to Family-Draining Bank Fees

When you’re trying to save for your family's future, the last thing you need is a bank account that chips away at your savings with unnecessary fees. Many traditional joint accounts come with monthly fees unless you maintain a high minimum balance. On top of that, you can get hit with charges for sending too many e-Transfers or, even worse, a painful non-sufficient funds (NSF) fee if you accidentally overspend.

KOHO was built to be a family-friendly, fee-free alternative.

  • No Monthly Fees: The standard KOHO account has no monthly maintenance fees. That’s more money that can go toward your family’s goals.
  • No NSF Fees, Ever: Because KOHO is a prepaid system, you can only spend the money you’ve loaded onto the account. This makes it impossible to overdraw and get charged an NSF fee, which can be over $45 per incident at a big bank. It’s a built-in safety net for your family’s budget.
  • Free e-Transfers: Need to pay the babysitter or send money to a family member? KOHO offers free and unlimited e-Transfers.

By eliminating these common fees, KOHO helps your family keep more of your hard-earned money.

Get Rewarded on Family Essentials

Families spend a lot on necessities. Groceries, gas, and household supplies make up a huge portion of the monthly budget. What if you could get money back on those essential purchases?

With a traditional debit card, you get no rewards. With KOHO, you get cashback on the things your family buys most.

  • Cashback on Groceries and Transportation: With a standard KOHO account, you earn 1% cashback every time you fill up the car or stock up at the supermarket.
  • Partner Offers for Bigger Savings: KOHO partners with hundreds of retailers across Canada, offering boosted cashback rates of up to 10%. Before you buy new shoes for the kids or book a family dinner, you can check the app for special offers to save even more.

Over a year, this cashback can add up to a significant amount—enough for a special family outing, a contribution to your vacation fund, or a little extra for the holiday season.

Save for Family Goals, Together

Saving for big goals—whether it’s a down payment on a house, a trip to Disney World, or your child's education—can feel daunting. KOHO provides the tools to make saving a collaborative and motivating process.

  • Create Shared Savings Goals: In the KOHO app, you can create specific Savings Goals for whatever your family is saving for. You can name your goal "Family Vacation" or "New Car Fund" and watch the progress bar grow as you contribute. This visual tracker makes saving feel more tangible and exciting for everyone.
  • Automate Your Savings with RoundUps: This feature is a game-changer for families. Every time you use your KOHO card, the app can automatically round up your purchase to the nearest dollar (or more) and move the spare change into your savings. That $4.50 morning coffee becomes a 50-cent contribution to your goal. It’s an effortless way for your family’s daily spending to fuel your long-term dreams.

KOHO turns saving from a chore into an automated, background process, helping your family build your nest egg faster than you thought possible.
