Say Goodbye to Pesky Foreign Transaction Fees

One of the most frustrating parts of spending money abroad is getting hit with foreign transaction (FX) fees. Most traditional credit and debit cards charge you a fee of around 2.5% on every single purchase you make in a different currency. It might not sound like much, but it adds up incredibly fast. If you spend $2,000 on your trip, that’s an extra $50 you’re paying in fees for no reason—money that could have been spent on a nice dinner or a memorable experience.

How KOHO Helps You Save

This is where KOHO really stands out for travelers. With a premium KOHO account, you can say goodbye to these annoying fees.

  • Zero Foreign Transaction Fees: With a KOHO Essential or Everything plan, you pay 0% in FX fees on all your purchases abroad. The app uses Mastercard’s competitive exchange rate, and that’s it. No extra 2.5% charge. This feature alone can save you a significant amount of money on any international trip.
  • Transparent Exchange: You’re not just saving money; you’re getting clarity. When you make a purchase, the KOHO app can show you the transaction in both the local currency and in Canadian dollars, so you always know exactly what you’re spending.

Imagine buying souvenirs, paying for train tickets, and enjoying local cuisine without having to do mental math to figure out how much extra your bank is charging you. That's the freedom KOHO gives you.

Travel with Enhanced Security and Peace of Mind

Keeping your money safe is a top priority for any traveler. Losing your wallet or having your card information stolen can turn a dream vacation into a nightmare. KOHO offers several layers of security that are far superior to carrying cash or using a traditional debit card.

A Firewall for Your Finances

  • A Separate Spending Account: Because KOHO is a reloadable account, it’s not directly linked to your main bank account where you keep your life savings. If your KOHO card were ever lost or compromised, a thief could only access the funds you’ve loaded onto it for your trip, not your entire savings. This acts as a crucial firewall, protecting your primary funds back home.
  • Instantly Lock Your Card: Misplaced your card after a long day of sightseeing? Don’t panic. You can instantly lock your card right from the KOHO app. If you find it a few minutes later in your bag, you can unlock it just as easily. This gives you immediate control and peace of mind that no one else can use your card.
  • Secure Virtual Cards: For booking flights, hotels, or tours online, you can create a secure virtual card within the app. This is a unique card number that is separate from your physical card, adding another layer of protection against online fraud.

With these security features, you can explore with confidence, knowing that your money is protected.
