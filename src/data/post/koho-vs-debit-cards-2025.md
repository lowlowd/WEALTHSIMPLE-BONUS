## How They Work: A Quick Refresher

**Debit card**: Directly linked to your primary chequing account at a bank. When you make a purchase, the money is instantly withdrawn from that account. It's your money, and you're spending it in real-time.

**KOHO card**: Reloadable prepaid Mastercard. You load money from your bank account onto your KOHO account, and then you spend from that loaded balance. It acts as a buffer between your spending and your main account.

This small separation is what unlocks a world of features designed to make you a smarter spender.
